Home

Welcome to my website.
Here, you will find pictures of some of my favourite Harry Potter characters.
I have made this website with the help of what I have learnt in my Coding Class.
There are three sections in this website out of which, the first one is home, which is this one.
The second and third ones are 'Grid View' and 'Slide View' respectively.
'Grid View' contains the images in a grid form and 'Slide View' has the pictures in a slider form
in which you have to click the 'Next' or 'Previous' arrow to see the next of previous picture respectively.
Select the required section name from the menu on top to navigate to each section.
